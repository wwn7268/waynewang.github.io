About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Mathematics and Statistics at South-Central Minzu University. My research focuses on machine learning systems, bioinformatics, and statistical computing. I am particularly interested in developing deep learning and statistical methods for single-cell RNA sequencing data analysis, as well as building efficient and scalable computational frameworks for biological data mining.

Before joining South-Central Minzu University, I conducted research in computational biology and machine learning, with work published in leading journals such as Genes and NAR Genomics and Bioinformatics. I am always looking for motivated students to join my research group. If you are interested in bioinformatics, statistical computing, or machine learning, please feel free to reach out.
